Scrambled Eggs

Gordon Ramsay's scrambled eggs

If there is one thing that grinds Gordon's gears, its overcooked scrambled eggs. This recipe will ensure that your scrambled eggs always come out fluffy and light. The main thing to keep in mind is eggs overcook very easily and even after you shut down the heat, the eggs will continue to cook in the hot pan. The key to the fluffy and lightness of the eggs is the constant stirring required in this recipe, so get ready for a workout.

Ingredients:

  • 6 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons creme fraiche
  • 3 chives, snipped

Steps:

  1. Break eggs into a pan on low heat
  2. Add half the butter
  3. Start stirring
  4. Frequently, remove pan on and off the heat whilst continuing to stir
  5. Repeat for 3-4 minutes, making sure the mixture doesn't set
  6. At the last minute, take the pan off the heat, add creme fraiche and chives while stirring
  7. Serve immediately
