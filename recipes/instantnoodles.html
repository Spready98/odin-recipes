Instant Ramyun Noodles

Shin Ramyun Noodles

Instant noodles are usually viewed as cheap, one-dimensional, non-healthy meals. However, there are simple ways to elevate the flavor and nutrition of instant noodles. It is important to get higher quality instant noodles, rather than the standard cup noodles. I recommend korean instant noodle brands such as Nongshim. We reboil the noodles after running them through the cold water so the texture of the noodles are more springy.

Ingredients:

  • Ramyun noodle pack
  • 1 egg
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Bring water to a boil and place noodles in, stir
  2. Continue to stir the noodles in boiling water until the noodles are seperated
  3. Strain the noodles through a strainer
  4. Run noodles under cold water
  5. Reboil another pot of water and put noodles back in
  6. Shortly after, strain 2/3 water from pot
  7. Add an egg and flavor packets into pot, stir
